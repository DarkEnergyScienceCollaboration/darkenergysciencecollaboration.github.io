Pan-STARRS Astrometric Survey and the Local Solar Neighborhood

Eugene Magnier

University of Hawaii/PanSTARRS PS1

Monday, December 15th, 2015 @ 4pm PST
Room C607 @ WRF Data Science Studio, 6th Floor, Physics-Astronomy Tower, University of Washington

Connect remotely with Google Hangouts or watch the broadcast on YouTube.

Abstract: The first science survey mission with Pan-STARRS 1 nears ready for public release. The 3pi survey component provides many repeated epochs on a cadence designed to optimize the measurement of parallax and proper motion of the red objects in the local solar neighborhood. I will discuss our efforts to characterize the low-mass populations using Pan-STARRS 1 proper motions, parallaxes and colors, including some of the challenges in beating down the systematic noise.
